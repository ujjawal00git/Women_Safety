Our Services

The Women's Safety Initiative (WSI) provides a range of services designed to support and protect women, ensuring their safety and promoting their rights.

Educational Programs

Workshops and Seminars

We conduct workshops on self-defense, legal rights, and mental health. Scheduled sessions across various locations, expert speakers and trainers, practical demonstrations, and interactive sessions.

Community Outreach

Programs aimed at educating communities about women's safety. Community events and forums, distribution of educational materials, collaboration with local organizations and leaders.

Advocacy and Policy Development

Policy Advocacy

We engage with policymakers to develop laws that protect women. Drafting and proposing new legislation, lobbying for policy changes, participation in governmental advisory committees.

Research

Conducting research to inform policy decisions and raise awareness. Studies on violence against women, data collection and analysis, publication of research findings.

Campaigns

National campaigns to raise awareness about women's safety. Media campaigns (TV, radio, online), public service announcements, collaboration with celebrities and influencers.

Collaboration and Partnerships

NGO Partnerships

Collaborations with NGOs to deliver comprehensive support services. Joint programs and initiatives, resource sharing, cross-training of staff.

Community Leaders

Working with community leaders to address local issues. Community-based safety programs, engagement with local councils and groups, advocacy for community-specific solutions.

Law Enforcement

Partnerships with law enforcement agencies to ensure effective response to women's safety issues. Training for law enforcement personnel, joint task forces, reporting and referral systems.

Additional Services

Complaint Services

Instructions on how to file a complaint, specific section for filing complaints against NRIs, and portal for users to check the status of their complaints. Confidentiality assurance and secure login.

E-Portal Services

Online services and resources available for women. Access to support resources, online consultations and appointments, digital educational materials.

NWC Staff Login

Secure login section for National Women's Commission staff members. Staff access to internal resources, secure communication channels, administrative tools and reports.

NWC Internship Opportunities

Information on internship programs available at the National Women's Commission. Details on application process, internship roles and responsibilities, benefits and opportunities for professional growth.

Support Services

Helpline

Our 24/7 helpline offers immediate assistance and support to women in distress. Confidential support, trained professionals available at all times, multi-lingual support.

Counseling

We provide professional counseling services to help women cope with trauma and emotional challenges. Individual and group sessions, trauma-informed care, support for emotional and psychological well-being.

Legal Aid

Our legal team offers free legal advice and representation to women facing legal issues. Assistance with legal documentation, representation in court, legal rights education and advice.
