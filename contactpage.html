Women's Safety Office

The Office of Women's Safety in Seoni, Madhya Pradesh, stands as a beacon of hope and empowerment for women across the region. Established with a mission to ensure the safety, dignity, and rights of women, this office provides comprehensive support services including a 24/7 helpline, professional counseling, and legal aid. Through educational programs, workshops, and community outreach initiatives, the office strives to raise awareness and foster a culture of respect and equality. Collaborating closely with local NGOs, community leaders, and law enforcement agencies, the Office of Women's Safety is dedicated to creating a secure and inclusive environment for all women. Located in the heart of Seoni, this office is a testament to the commitment of Madhya Pradesh towards women's welfare and empowerment.
